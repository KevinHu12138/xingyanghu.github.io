Face Super Resolution (DMFSR)

Project Type: Deep Learning / Computer Vision / Research

Technology Stack: Python, PyTorch, U-Net, Diffusion Models

This project introduces DMFSR, a Deep-learning based Multi-stage Face Super Resolution algorithm. It leverages a multi-stage diffusion model combined with a U-Net architecture to reconstruct high-resolution facial images from severely degraded low-resolution inputs.

DMFSR significantly outperforms existing GAN-based methods on benchmark datasets such as CelebA and FFHQ, particularly in terms of PSNR and SSIM. It preserves detailed facial structures and shows strong generalization on both synthetic and real-world low-resolution images.

I was responsible for model design, loss function tuning, training strategy implementation, and evaluation. This project demonstrates the effectiveness of combining denoising diffusion with facial prior learning.
