Bionic Snake Robot

Project Type: Robotics / Biomechanics / Undergraduate Research

Technology Stack: Embedded Control, Servo Motor XH540-W270-R, Python, Mechanical Design

This project presents a bionic snake-like robot built using XH540-W270-R servos and an orthogonal steering gear, designed with a unique mechanical shell structure for easier control and greater flexibility in movement.

The robot employs lateral and spiral rolling gaits for vertical climbing. Experimental results confirmed that the motion patterns align with torque behaviors of each joint. I was responsible for mechanical assembly, gait design, control programming, and experimental testing.

The project provided hands-on experience in robotic actuation and mechanical coordination, helping deepen my understanding of bio-inspired design and motion planning.
