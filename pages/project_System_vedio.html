Robotic Systems for Sustainable Development Goals

Project Type: Research Video / Thematic Analysis / Public Communication

Technology Scope: Humanoid Robots, Construction Robots, Industrial Automation

In this project, I created a research-based video that explores the relationship between robotic technologies and the United Nations Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs). The project involved identifying key areas where robotics can directly support global objectives such as quality education, sustainable industry, and climate action.

Through video editing and data storytelling, I illustrated how industrial, humanoid, and construction robots can contribute to sustainable development. This helped me deepen my understanding of robotics in the context of real-world impact and global responsibility.
