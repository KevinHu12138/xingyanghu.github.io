Research Interests

Humanoid robot

Humanoid robots are robots designed to resemble humans and are used in areas like healthcare, education, and customer service. Equipped with sensors, cameras, and AI, they can interact with people, assist in tasks, and work in hazardous environments. Their technology includes actuators for movement and AI for decision-making. As these robots advance, they are expected to play bigger roles in personal assistance, entertainment, and disaster response, providing human-like interaction and adapting to complex tasks.
This is a very promising project, and I want to study it further

Construction Robot

Construction robots assist in tasks like bricklaying, welding, and material transport, using sensors, cameras, and AI for efficiency and safety. With actuators for precise movement and AI for decision-making, they work in challenging environments. As technology evolves, these robots are expected to reduce labor costs, increase speed, and take on roles like 3D printing and building inspection.
This is a very promising project, and I want to study it further

Face super resolution

In this project,I propose DMFSR, a deep learning-based face super-resolution algorithm that uses a multi-stage diffusion model combined with a U-Net architecture to enhance low-resolution face images. DMFSR outperforms existing GAN-based methods in PSNR and SSIM metrics, achieving superior results on both synthetic and real datasets with strong practical robustness.

Bionic snake robot

This project presents a bionic snake-like robot built using XH540-W270-R servos and an orthogonal steering gear, designed with a unique mechanical shell structure for easier control. The robot employs lateral and spiral rolling gaits for vertical climbing, with experimental results confirming that the motion aligns with the torque behavior of each joint.
